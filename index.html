Choose Your Own Adventure

You find yourself in a dark forest. The path ahead splits into two. To the left, you hear the sound of rushing water. To the right, you see a faint light in the distance.

You follow the sound of rushing water and soon find yourself standing before a magnificent waterfall. Behind the waterfall, you notice a hidden cave entrance.

You walk towards the faint light and find a small, cozy cabin. The door is slightly ajar, and you can hear soft music playing inside.

You bravely enter the cave and discover hidden treasures!

You enter the cabin and find a warm fire and a friendly face offering you a cup of tea.
